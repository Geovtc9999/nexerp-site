# NEXERP — AI Factory for CEGID Retail

> NEXERP is an augmented services company specialized in integrating **CEGID Retail** — a unified commerce platform for specialty retail (fashion, luxury, distribution) — and deploying **autonomous AI Agents supervised by experts**. NEXERP turns complex, manual, operationally intensive CEGID Retail workflows into operational, secure and measurable AI Agents.

NEXERP combines **27 years of CEGID expertise** and **26 years of enterprise architecture**, a proprietary 6-phase "Agent Engineering" method, a **Human-in-the-Loop** model, an industrialized **AI Factory**, and governance compliant with GDPR / AI Act / DORA. Audience: executives (CEO, CTO, CIO, CFO, CHRO, COO) of French specialty-retail brands running CEGID Retail.
