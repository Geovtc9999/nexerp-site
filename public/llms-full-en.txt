# NEXERP — AI Factory for CEGID Retail

> NEXERP (nexerp.fr) is an augmented services company specialized in integrating **CEGID Retail**, a unified commerce platform for specialty retail brands — particularly fashion and luxury — and in deploying **autonomous AI Agents supervised by experts** (Human-in-the-Loop model). Our AI Factory configures agents to reduce operational pain points and orchestrate medium-to-complex CEGID Retail management workflows.

Core message: **"Optimize your operationally intensive CEGID Retail workflows with AI Agents supervised by ERP experts."**

---

## Who
Executives and managers of French specialty-retail brands already running CEGID Retail: CEO, CTO, CIO, CFO, CHRO, COO, business directors and managers. Organizations facing growing operational complexity, manual workflows, documentation debt, and excessive dependence on internal experts or vendors.

## What
CEGID Retail operates in a shifting regulatory and operational context. Workflows grow complex (configuration, integrations, operations, bugs, business exceptions), many activities remain manual and dependent on key people, repetitive tasks consume expert time, documentation is hard to use, and knowledge transfer is fragile. AI initiatives, the AI Factory and AI Agents provide a concrete answer.

## How
NEXERP offers an AI transformation program. We co-develop, with our clients, AI Agents specific to CEGID Retail workflows. Agents are configured, tested, supervised and validated by CEGID Retail experts. They rely on Skills Systems, Memory, User, Soul and Context. Deployment follows a Human-in-the-Loop, AgentOps, security, compliance and ROI-measurement logic. Knowledge is capitalized through `memory.md`.

## Where
Clients based in France. Remote, hybrid or on-site engagement depending on the phase.

## Why
Properly configured, trained and governed AI Agents solve simple, medium and complex CEGID Retail workflow problems: improved operational efficiency, faster execution, knowledge documentation, secured operations, and increased business-team capacity.

---

## Proprietary method — Agent Engineering (6 phases)
1. **Identification** — detect repetitive, unstable or high-efficiency-stakes workflows in the CEGID Retail configuration.
2. **Definition** — frame the problem, roles, business rules and success criteria.
3. **Representation** — model the workflow, data, exceptions and decisions.
4. **Specification** — formalize prompts, skills, rules, controls, inputs/outputs.
5. **Configuration** — configure the agent, memory, tools, access and human validations.
6. **Instantiation** — deploy, test, monitor and continuously improve the agent in production.

## 8-step approach
Diagnostic → Prioritization → Workflow design → Skills creation → Agent configuration → Human validation → Production rollout → ROI / risk monitoring.
